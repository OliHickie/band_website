The UK's Original Rock 'n' Roll Oompah Band

Education

“Their brilliance brought tears to my eyes. Such laughter and fun... Today, our school was richer in spirit hearing them perform.”

Mrs M. Meyers Head of Music, Finton House School

Oompah Brass have a unique approach to creating bespoke education projects - ranging from assembly demonstrations to huge collaborative ensembles. They have entertained and inspired hundreds (if not thousands!) of young people across the UK to take up a brass instrument, and twice been awarded a grant from the London Music Fund.

Playing brass instruments is terrific fun - especially when playing in a group. Our in-house arranger can create versions of our classic pop tunes with students in mind so that everyone can become part of the band, beginner or expert!

To find out more, or to start creating a bespoke project for your school or organisation, drop us a line!

Recent Projects

The Oompah Project

A huge project in conjunction with the Mayor's Music Fund, Waltham Forest and Redbridge Music Services.

120 beginner brass players joined an advanced brass band and Oompah Brass to form a giant band, performing bespoke arrangements including some Bonnie Tyler and Guns N' Roses!

THAMES 10th Anniversary Concert

Tower Hamlets Arts and Music Education Service held their 10 year anniversary concert at the Barbican and invited Oompah Brass worked with students from the London Borough to perform bespoke arrangements.

Oompah Brass provided music to be practiced in advance, and led rehearsals at the Saturday Music Center and the Barbican.
